07 — Design Principles

Guiding Rules

◎
Instrument, Not Toy
Every element should feel purposeful and precise. No decorative flourishes, no playful illustrations. This is a tool for professionals.
◑
Density With Clarity
Pack information tightly but keep it scannable. Use typography weight, color hierarchy, and spacing to create structure.
◆
Signal, Not Noise
Amber is the one accent color. It means "look here." Overusing it dilutes its power. Most of the UI is neutral.
◯
Zero Configuration
Immediately usable. No sign-up, no onboarding wizard. Open it, paste a URL, hit Send.
